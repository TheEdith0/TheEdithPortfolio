# Lumière — Ceiling Lamp Showcase
Context & Project Overview

## Description
A 3D interactive web showcase for a premium ceiling lamp ("Lumière") and a dynamic glitch/cyberpunk ending sequence. The page features a cinematic scroll-driven animation where the camera pulls back, descends with a falling sword, and concludes in a glitch-corrupted holographic environment.

## Core Features & Visuals
1. **3D Models**: 
   - "Plafonnier couloir RdC" ceiling lamp loaded via GLTFLoader with Draco compression.
   - `sword.glb` model that spawns mid-scroll and drops down.
2. **Lighting & Post-Processing**:
   - `UnrealBloomPass` for premium glowing effects and bloom.
   - `GlitchPass` (from three.js addons) triggered at the end of the scroll for a corrupted aesthetic.
3. **Particle Systems**:
   - **Volumetric Beam**: A light cone emanating from the lamp.
   - **Helix Trails**: Glowing streaks spiraling downward.
   - **Digital Embers**: Neon pink and blue sparks rising from the bottom floor.
   - **Ambient Dust & Stars**: Drifting particles and background space.

## Critical Project Constraints & Logic Flow
**(Always check these rules before modifying code to prevent breaking established visuals):**

1. **Sword Sequence (`sword.glb`)**:
   - A glowing precursor particle falls from the lamp starting at 22% scroll.
   - At 32% scroll, the particle erupts into the `sword.glb` model.
   - **Camera Constraints**: The camera MUST NEVER dive down to the physical floor (`Y = -35`). The camera remains high (`Y ≈ 1.5`) and tilts down to look at the bottom of the light beam (`beamEndY`).
   - **Sword Descent Limit**: Because the camera stays high, the sword stops at `beamEndY - 2.0` (mid-air) so it remains perfectly in frame.

2. **Sword Materials & Lighting**:
   - **Glow Effect**: The sword material is given a soft yellow emissive tint, and a yellow `PointLight` is attached directly to the sword blade so the metallic edges catch a persistent glowing warm light as it rotates.

3. **The Bottom Scene (90% -> 100% Scroll)**:
   - **Impact Shockwave**: Two expanding golden rings anchor at the sword's stop point (`beamEndY - 2.0`).
   - **Holographic Cyber-Grid**: A custom GLSL ShaderMaterial plane (`cyberGridMesh`) renders a neon pink grid with a radial fade. It lies perfectly flat on the ground.
   - **Digital Embers**: 300 neon red and blue sparks (`embersMesh`) rise upwards from the grid.
   - **Cinematic Letterboxing**: HTML/CSS pure black bars (`#cinema-bar-top`, `#cinema-bar-bottom`) slide in from off-screen to frame the sequence.
   - **Glitch Overlay**: The `GlitchPass` and an HTML glitch overlay (`#glitch-overlay`) activate heavily.

## Development Workflow
- When adding to the bottom section, remember it relies heavily on CSS/HTML overlays synchronized with the `sp` (scroll progress) variable in the `animate()` loop.
- Any 3D objects meant for the "floor" should be anchored to `beamEndY - 2.0`.
