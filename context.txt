# Lumière — Ceiling Lamp Showcase
Context & Project Overview

## Description
A 3D interactive web showcase for a premium ceiling lamp ("Lumière"), built using Three.js. The page features a cinematic scroll-driven animation where the camera zooms out, orbits around the lamp, and creates an illusion of the lamp rising as the user scrolls down the page.

## Core Features & Visuals
1. **3D Model**: The "Plafonnier couloir RdC" ceiling lamp is loaded via `GLTFLoader` with Draco compression.
2. **Lighting & Post-Processing**:
   - Environment map (PMREM equirectangular).
   - PointLight (flickering bulb) and SpotLight.
   - `UnrealBloomPass` for premium glowing effects and bloom.
3. **Particle Systems (God Rays & Ambient)**:
   - **Volumetric Beam / God Rays**: A cone of light emanating from the lamp downwards.
   - **Animated Helix Trails**: 3 glowing comet-like streaks that spiral downward along the beam in a helix path.
   - **Ambient Particles**: Drifting dust particles and static background stars.
4. **Scroll-Driven Animation**:
   - **Phase 1 (Zoom Out, 0–38% scroll)**: The camera smoothly pulls back from a close-up of the lamp.
   - **Phase 2 (Orbit & Rise, 38–100% scroll)**: The camera orbits 270° around the scene while the lamp and its attached particle systems move upwards.

## V5 Additions & Critical Project Constraints 
**(Always check these rules before modifying code to prevent breaking established visuals):**

1. **Sword Sequence (`sword.glb`)**:
   - A glowing precursor particle falls from the lamp starting at 22% scroll.
   - At 32% scroll, the particle erupts into the `sword.glb` model.
   - **Camera Constraints**: The camera MUST NEVER dive down to the physical floor (`Y = -35`). The camera remains high (`Y ≈ 1.5`) and tilts down to look at the bottom of the light beam (`beamEndY`).
   - **Sword Descent Limit**: Because the camera stays high, the sword stops at `beamEndY - 2.0` (mid-air) so it remains perfectly in frame. It does NOT drop to the glass floor.
2. **Sword Materials & Lighting**:
   - **PRESERVE ORIGINAL TEXTURES**: The GLTF materials for the sword are untouched (no overriding `color` or `metalness`).
   - **Golden Edge Glow**: Achieved using two external `PointLight` objects (`bladeLight1`, `bladeLight2`) that perfectly track the sword's Y-position, casting golden light onto the metallic blades from the outside.
3. **Impact Shockwave**:
   - Two expanding golden rings (`shockwaveGroup`) trigger from 90% -> 100% scroll.
   - **Positioning**: They are anchored exactly at the sword's stopping point (`beamEndY - 2.0`), NOT the physical floor, ensuring they are visible to the camera.
   - **Visibility**: The rings fade IN and stay visible at 100% scroll (opacity does not drop to 0 at the end).
